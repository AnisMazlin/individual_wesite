MY LIFETIME EXPERIENCE

My first time watching movie at cinema was when I studied at Kolej Matrikulasi Melaka. I went to GSC Cinema Dataran Pahlawan.

I watched movie named "Skyscraper" with my other three roommates. It was a great first-time experience which I wish will happen again sooner or later.

My first waterpark I went when I was younger is at Wet World Air Panas Pedas Resort in Negeri Sembilan.

I was brought by my youngest auntie and she treated all her nieces and nephews since it was our first outing together.

My another first time which I will not forget for the rest of my life was when I went to watch a football match in Kota Bharu.

The match was between Kelantan FA and Kedah FA. I was so happy because I got to see my favourite football player and thankfully they won the match.

I still have the entrance ticket to enter the stadium in my storage box.

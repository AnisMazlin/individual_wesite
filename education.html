MY EDUCATION BACKGROUND

I first started my kindergarten at Tabika Kemas, TUDM Gong Kedak, Jerteh.

Next, I started primary school at Sekolah Kebangsaan Kampung Baharu for just one year.

Then, I finished my Standard 2 until Standard 6 at Sekolah Kebangsaan Nik Daud, Jakar.

Since I only got 4A's for my UPSR examination, my father sent me to Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Jeli for its "Kelas Rancangan Khas".

I got 9A's for PT3 examination and I decided to change my secondary school because I did not want to stay at the hostel.

I continued my another two years of secondary school at Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Bukit Bunga and finally got my SPM results.

After the secondary school, I went to Kolej Matrikulasi Melaka for one year.

And now, I am at UiTM Machang in completing my degree.
